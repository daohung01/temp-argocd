# Archived template snapshot

This folder is an **archived snapshot** of the apps-of-apps development template.

- **Use**: Copy this folder into your repo, or extract from `template-dev.tar.gz` (if provided alongside), then follow `README.md` and replace placeholders.
- **Frozen**: No further changes are expected; use as reference or starting point.
- **Date archived**: 2025-03-04

To adopt: copy `template-dev` to your deployments repo (e.g. under `ops/argocd-deployment/` or your Git path), update repo URLs and tenant/namespace placeholders, then apply `root-app-of-apps.yaml` from your ArgoCD bootstrap.
